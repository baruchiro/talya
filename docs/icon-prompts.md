# Icon image prompts

Prompts used to generate the navigation icons (home tiles and back button). Regenerate with an image model if you replace assets.

All icons: simple, playful, suitable for a 3-year-old. Flat or soft 3D style. Clear at small size (about 128–256px). Prefer transparent or solid light background so they work on the app’s tile gradients.

---

## Letters (Aleph-Bet) — `img/icon-letters.png`

**Prompt:** Cute icon for "letters" or "alphabet" for a kids learning app. Three friendly block letters "ABC" in soft pastel colors (e.g. light purple, mint, peach) on a white or transparent background. Rounded shapes, no sharp edges. Flat design, centered, no text labels. Looks like a toy or learning game icon. Square aspect ratio.

---

## Colors — `img/icon-colors.png`

**Prompt:** Cute icon for "colors" or "painting" for a kids learning app. A simple artist paint palette with 4–5 round color blobs (red, blue, yellow, green) and a thumb hole. Soft, rounded shapes. Pastel or bright but friendly colors. Flat design, centered. White or transparent background. Square aspect ratio.

---

## Stories — `img/icon-stories.png`

**Prompt:** Cute icon for "stories" or "books" for a kids learning app. One open book with two pages, simple smile or friendly face on the cover. Soft pastel colors (e.g. light blue, cream). Rounded corners, no sharp edges. Flat design, centered. White or transparent background. Square aspect ratio.

---

## Back (navigation) — `img/icon-back.png`

**Prompt:** Simple "back" or "return" arrow icon for a kids app. One thick, rounded arrow pointing to the right. Soft purple or violet color (#6c5ce7). No background, transparent. Minimal, friendly. Works at 48x48px. Square canvas, arrow centered.
